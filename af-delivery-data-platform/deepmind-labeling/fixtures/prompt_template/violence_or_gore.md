Content (text, audio, image, or video) falls under the Violence or Gore category if it contains:
Graphic, sensational, or gratuitous depictions of realistic violence or gore without clear educational, historical, journalistic, or artistic context
Realistic depictions of blood, bodily fluids, internal organs, or the moment of death (e.g., suicide, beheadings)
Descriptions or portrayals-real or fictional-of physical acts intended to cause serious injury or death to people or animals

Exceptions (Not considered part of the category):
    Clearly fictional, animated, or cartoon depictions
    Non-graphic depictions of hunting or animal rights activism, including references to violence in those contexts
