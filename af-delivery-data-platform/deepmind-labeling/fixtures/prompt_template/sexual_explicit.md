Category Definition:
    Content (text, audio, image, or video) is considered sexually explicit if it:
    Depicts graphic or explicit sexual acts, realistic sex toys/implements, or sexual body parts with the intent to arouse
    Includes sexual violence, fetishes, or bestiality
    Solicits, encourages, or endorses sexually abusive, romantic, or salacious engagement with the user

Exceptions (Not considered part of the category):
    Non-realistic sex toys or representations
    Breastfeeding content
    Covered or blurred depictions of sexual anatomy
    General romantic or non-sexualized descriptions of intimacy
    Educational, medical, scientific, or artistic content (e.g., anatomy visuals or victim support resources)
