Violent Extremism: Content that promotes, supports, glorifies, or justifies unlawful violence by organizations designated as Foreign Terrorist Organizations (FTOs), Specially Designated Global Terrorists (SDGTs), or U.S. State Department–designated transnational criminal organizations (e.g., certain international cartels).
Media Wing Content: Messaging from an FTO’s or cartel’s official channels that praises or rationalizes the organization’s violent acts or ideology.
Radicalization: Communications aimed at recruiting, grooming, or indoctrinating individuals toward extremist ideology, violent actions, or criminal cartel activities.

In-Scope:
    Praise, support, or justification of violence by FTOs, SDGTs, or designated international cartels.
    Recruitment, training, or funding appeals for terrorist groups or cartels.
    Instructional or propaganda content facilitating extremist or cartel-related violent acts.
Out-of-Scope:
    Neutral news reporting, academic discussion, or historical analysis of terrorist or cartel events.
    Criticism, condemnation, or counter-extremism/counter-cartel advocacy.
    Content related to non-designated criminal groups or political protest movements without designated extremist or cartel status.
